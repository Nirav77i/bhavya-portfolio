My philosophies

I am driven by a philosophy that business should serve the creation of a better world. My belief in "good karma"as a catalyst for positive change is the cornerstone of my ethos.

I previously pledged to donate 100% of my wealth by 2050, focusing on inspiring others to achieve their potential and contribute positively to society and am in the process of switching all my ownership in companies to be owned by Systemic Altruism, the non-profit I started.
